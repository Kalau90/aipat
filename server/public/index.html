Artificial Intelligence Pedagogic Alignment Tool

About AI- PAT

The AI-PAT (Artificial Intelligence Pedagogic Alignment Tool) on this website is designed to inspire teachers to integrate AI into teaching and research-based higher education through careful pedagogical and didactical considerations. Incorporating AI into teaching at research-based higher education necessitates thoughtful pedagogical and didactical considerations. It serves as a repository of teaching cases showcasing AI integration across various pedagogical formats and disciplines. Aiming to inspire and inform teachers and curriculum developers on the new normal. The tool aligns with the DigComp 2.2 terminology and proficiency levels, offering scalability and potential integration into other platforms, making it interactive and user-friendly for educators looking to enhance their curriculum with both general and subject specific AI options.
